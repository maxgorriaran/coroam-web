# "How it Works" - Premium/Active Flow

The Coroam experience begins with intent: What’s your vibe today? Whether it’s Historic Lore or Foodie Favorites, you set the tone. Our AI provides intelligent, non-intrusive nudges as you wander, but stays completely flexible—pivot mid-roam to explore fresh interests for instant, curated options.

It’s more than a map; it’s a living diary. Document your 'First Finds' with photos and notes as you go, then wrap up with a shared summary that transforms a simple walk into a documented adventure. Input curiosity. Output discovery.
